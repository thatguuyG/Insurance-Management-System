Travel Insurance

Simple and flexible travel Insurance. Buy at home or while travelling, and claim online from anywhere in the world

Why Travel Insurance

  • Travel insurance is a “Peace of mind” for anyone travelling abroad. Everyone loves to go on holidays and there is definitely more to think about when traveling overseas to a foreign country. The biggest fear people have when travelling is falling seriously ill in a place where they don’t know the language, people, or customs.

  • It not only covers the costs of unforeseen incidents, but it also provides practical assistance when you are abroad – 24 hours a day, 365 days a year!
Product highlights
  • Worldwide cover 24 hours a day, 365 days a year

  • Emergency Medical and Related Expenses

  • Attractive Student cover.

  • Family rates for you and your children to help keep travel cost down
