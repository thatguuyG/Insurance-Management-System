Life Insurance

Pays a tax-free lump sum to your beneficiaries in the event of death

TERM OPTION
This benefit pays a lump sum in the event of your death. The policy can be taken for a specific term (minimum of 10 years).

Features and benefits

  • This cover pays a lump sum to your nominated beneficiary.
  • No taxes apply on claims
  • A claim can take up to seven working days from receipt of all required supporting claims documentation.

    • WHOLE LIFE OPTION

    This benefit pays a lump sum in the event of your death. This policy is for whole of life.
Features and benefits
  • This cover pays a lump sum to your nominated beneficiary.

  • The policy is not payable if death is brought about or accelerated by suicide or by the exclusions specifically stated in your policy.

  • No taxes apply on claims.
